Our Team

Meet the experts dedicated to your success.

Mark Bridge

Managing Partner

Mark Bridge is a founding partner at 4Legal with over 20 years of experience in corporate law and strategic litigation. He has successfully represented Fortune 500 companies and high-net-worth individuals in complex disputes. Mark is known for his analytical approach and unwavering dedication to achieving favorable outcomes for his clients.

Before establishing 4Legal, Mark served as a senior prosecutor, giving him a unique perspective on both sides of the legal system. He is a frequent speaker at national legal conferences and a mentor to young attorneys.

Maria Tucker

Senior Associate

Maria Tucker specializes in civil rights and employment law, advocating tirelessly for workplace fairness and individual freedoms. Her empathetic yet assertive style has earned her a reputation as a fierce defender of justice.

With a background in public interest law, Maria has led several high-profile class-action lawsuits. She is passionate about community outreach and regularly provides pro bono legal services to underserved populations.

Antony Johnson

Partner, Criminal Defense

Antony Johnson leads the criminal defense division at 4Legal. With a decade of trial experience, Antony is committed to protecting the rights of the accused and ensuring fair legal proceedings.

His track record includes numerous acquittals and reduced sentences in high-stakes cases. Antony's meticulous preparation and courtroom presence make him a formidable opponent.

Roseline Belfield

Partner, Family Law

Roseline Belfield brings compassion and expertise to family law matters, including divorce, child custody, and estate planning. She understands the emotional complexities of these cases and strives to guide clients with sensitivity and clarity.

Her approach focuses on mediation and amicable resolutions whenever possible, but she is prepared to litigate vigorously when necessary to protect her clients' interests.
